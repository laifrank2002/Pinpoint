Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of pinpoint shirts?

Our shirts are designed for adventure minded folk who want to explore Toronto. Of course, they also look nice, and you can just buy them as a shirt.

Speaking of buying, can I order your shirts online?

Unfortunately, we are a JA company that is only doing limited runs of our product. Delivery is just unaffordable for us. However, you can buy them in store at the RBC Grangeway.

What does JA stand for?

I'm glad you asked! JA stands for Junior Achievement. They help young entrepeurneurs like us in starting their own business. This is JA Canada.
